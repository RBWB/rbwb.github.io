Delivery Policy

A local delivery fee of 3000Kyats in local town(Nay Pyi Taw) plus an additinal fee to other towns.
There may be an additinal fee added for certain holidays.
Same-Day delivery may not always be available. Local delivery orders must be received before 10am,
Monday through Friday, and 9am Saturday in the recipient's time zone to assure same-day delivery when available.
Orders received after that time will be delivered the following day or may require a special delivery charge
to be taken out the same day if there is a driver available.
We will do our best to accommodate deliveries at specific times of the day,
but we cannot guarantee it without a special delivery charge as our driver will have to re-route all our daily deliveries.
Please include a contact phone number where we can reach you on the day of delivery.
Should questions arise, we may need to contact you before delivery can be completed.
Please also include the recipient's phone number in ADDITION to your own. We are not able to gurantee
successful delivery without a working contact phone number for both the recipient and the sender of flowers.
No orders of fresh flowers or any other gift will be left at recipient's address without a signature unless we are
specifically instructed to and weather conditions permit. We cannot leave flowers during inclement weather and
in temperatures below 55 degrees or above 75 degrees due to their perishable nature. We will be happy to accept
your incoming international orders for delivery in Ann Arbor. Please make sure to include a working email address as
we will need this to contact you if any issues arise.
